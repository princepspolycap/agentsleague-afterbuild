# Carlotta Pitch Draft

Hey Carlotta,

We are locking our Reasoning Agents submission around the live-battle RPG scaffold, but applying it to a business-building workflow: **Your Company Is the Dungeon**.

The idea is a side-scrolling, multi-agent experience where a founder enters a business pitch, a Foundry-powered Master Narrator decomposes it into a quest line, and specialist character agents such as Strategist, Designer, and Marketer execute steps that produce real artifacts. The player reviews and approves each artifact before XP is awarded.

It still follows the challenge architecture closely: the Master Narrator maps to the Game Master, the specialist agents map to the RPG party, Foundry IQ grounds the system in business-launch knowledge, code tools validate outputs, and the UI makes the reasoning flow visible during the demo.

The goal is to make the RPG pattern useful beyond entertainment: a forkable "boot.dev for building a business" that developers can clone and adapt into their own company or workflow dungeon.
